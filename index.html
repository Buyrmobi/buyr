Why Buyr?

Buyr is designed around you. You buy what you want, when you want it, at the price that makes most sense to you – all from a seller in your locality. Make buying hassle-free and super convenient with Buyr.

You call the shots

Yes you read it right. With Buyr, you are empowered to shop and buy the way you want to. Simply state what you want along with your budget and timeline and sit back. Sellers contact you with the best deals they have on hand. If you wish, negotiate with the sellers through Buyr.

Compare and choose

You get to choose the deal you like from among a range of great deals tailor-made to your buys. You do not have to step into shop after shop and be subjected to endless sales spiels. You know what you want – simply sit at home and let Buyr handle the rest.

Buy your way

Once you have chosen the deal that best fits your requirements, you can, if you wish, get the touch and feel of the product before you buy. You can also request the seller to send it directly to your residence. Buy your way and always get the best deal in town.

One app for many buys

Experience the simplicity. Choose products from the different categories, set the budget and time-to-buy. Submit your request. Sit back, relax and let the sellers respond instantly with terrific deals. Choose your seller and complete your buy!
